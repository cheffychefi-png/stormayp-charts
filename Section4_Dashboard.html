⚠️ Equity Finding
Data centers are frequently located near lower-income communities and communities of color that already face higher exposure to environmental hazards and have less access to air conditioning and green spaces to offset rising temperatures (EPA, 2023). In Virginia alone, data centers consumed 26% of the state's total electricity in 2023 — and all of that energy eventually became heat felt by surrounding communities (Pew Research Center, 2025). The communities least responsible for the rise of AI are often the ones most directly feeling its heat.
